# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

We expect respectful, professional collaboration.

- Be constructive and specific.
- Focus on ideas, code, and outcomes.
- Avoid personal attacks, harassment, or discrimination.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or intimidation.
- Hate speech or discriminatory behavior.
- Deliberate disruption of project discussion.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove content or limit participation for violations.

## Reporting

If you experience or observe unacceptable behavior, report it privately to maintainers.
