# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Practical designers and creators on Windows devices, including those on low-end hardware. They need a fast, reliable, and lightweight tool for quick image editing tasks.

## Product Purpose
A lightweight desktop image editor that provides a familiar professional workflow with a distinct identity. Success means providing a "slim & snappy" experience that feels like a high-precision tool without the weight of traditional suites.

## Brand Personality
Precise, Snappy, Professional.
Voice: Direct and confident.
Tone: Technical but modern and approachable.
Emotional goals: Speed, precision, confidence.

## Anti-references
- "AI Slop" (generic glassmorphism, overused blurs, deep floating SaaS shadows).
- Bloated "all-in-one" design suites.
- Web-centric UI patterns that feel out of place in a native desktop app.

## Design Principles
1. **Mechanical Precision with Native Feel**: Structural panels at 0px, interactive elements at 2-4px, elevated surfaces at 6px. Radius communicates hierarchy, not decoration.
2. **Technical Neutrality**: Zero-tint grays to ensure color accuracy on the canvas.
3. **Slim & Snappy**: Minimize visual noise (less borders, purposeful negative space) to maximize performance and focus.
4. **Tool-First Affordance**: Clear visual feedback for interactive elements without excessive decoration.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- WCAG 2.1/2.2 Level AA compliance (specifically Contrast 1.4.3 and Focus Visible 2.4.7).
- Target size compliance for desktop (min 24x24px).
- Robust keyboard navigation (WCAG2ICT Guidance).
